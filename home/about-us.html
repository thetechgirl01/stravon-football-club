About Geezerz FC

Passion. Power. Pride. Our story through the years

Our Story

Founded in 1992, Geezerz FC began as a small local club with big dreams. What started as a group of passionate football enthusiasts playing in local parks has grown into one of the most respected clubs in the nation.

Our journey has been marked by perseverance, community support, and an unwavering commitment to excellence both on and off the pitch. From our early days in regional leagues to our current position competing at the highest levels, every step has been fueled by the dedication of our players, staff, and fans.

Today, we stand proud with multiple league titles, domestic cups, and memorable European campaigns that have etched our name in football history.

Our Core Values

Community

We believe football has the power to unite people. Our club is deeply rooted in our local community, with programs that support youth development, education, and social inclusion.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in everything we do - from our first team performances to our academy development and fan experience. Good enough is never enough.

Integrity

We play fair, respect our opponents, and honor the game. Our success is measured not just by trophies but by how we achieve them and the positive impact we create.

Our Home

The Geezerz Stadium, with its 45,000 capacity, is more than just a football ground - it's a fortress where memories are made. Opened in 2005, our state-of-the-art facility has hosted countless iconic matches and unforgettable moments.

With world-class facilities for players and fans alike, our stadium has been consistently rated as one of the best matchday experiences in the league. The famous "Yellow Wall" stand creates an atmosphere that inspires our team and intimidates opponents.

Geezerz Academy

Developing Future Stars

Our youth academy is the lifeblood of the club, producing talented players who embody the Geezerz spirit. With Category 1 status, we provide world-class coaching and education for boys and girls aged 8-18.

Over 60% of our first-team squad are academy graduates, a testament to our commitment to developing homegrown talent. Our holistic approach focuses on technical, tactical, physical, and psychological development.
